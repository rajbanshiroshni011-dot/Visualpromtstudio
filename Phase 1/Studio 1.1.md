## Prompt : Create a realistic acrylic painting of four people sitting on a park bench, each looking down at their phones and not interacting with one another. In the background, children are playing and trees are visible. The artwork should serve as a social protest about phone addiction and digital disconnection. The style should be representational, with natural lighting and realistic human proportions. The composition should clearly show physical closeness but emotional distance.
## Vocabulary Breakdown :
Medium: Acrylic painting 
Meaning: To draw attention to the emotional detachment brought on by heavy phone use
Subject matter: Four prople sitting together on a bench looking at their phones not talking to anyone, while on the other hand life continues around them.
Style: Representational Art.
